Expense Tracking Dashboard can involve various modules such as:

Summary and Key Metrics – Overview of expenses, income, and balances.

Expense Breakdown – Detailed view of all expenses with categories, dates, and amounts.

Recurring Expenses – Show recurring bills or expenses (e.g., rent, utilities).

Expense Trends – Visualize the spending patterns over time (e.g., line charts).

Category Analysis – Pie chart showing the breakdown of expenses by category.

Payment History – Track all payments made and their status.

Notifications and Reminders – Show overdue payments or upcoming bills.

Budget Tracking – Monitor how the user is tracking against their budget.

Search and Filters – Ability to filter or search for specific expenses by date, category, or amount.
